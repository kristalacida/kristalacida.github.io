Why I want to work with Canva

Canva’s vision of helping non-designers create beautiful images is similar with what I want to do as a designer. I’ve recommended using Canva to my previous co-workers who are in charge of managing the social media accounts of the companies I have worked for. Ever since Design School started, I realized I wanted to work for Canva. Design trends evolve rapidly and I would like to work together with the amazing designers ( I’ve stalked some on Dribbble ). By working alongside these talented people, I would be able to further develop my own set of skills and help the company in its vision to teach people how to create beautiful things.

Why should Canva hire me

My dream to teach and provide design for others coincides with Canva’s vision. This becomes my inspiration as I start creating designs following the company’s branding and guidelines. Ever since I started working as a designer, I have been exposed to a wide range of clients in different fields. These include designing for Medical companies, Real Estate, Child Care Centers, Fashion, Games, Startups, etc. Because of this, I am able to adapt my work’s personality according to what the client needs.

Aside from that, I am quite good at being unique.
